Outreach

Since 2022, I’ve been teaching introductory and intermediate Python programming to high school students at Greyhills Academy High School in Tuba City, Arizona. I’ve been volunteering because I care about helping expand access to coding and STEM education in Indigenous communities through ongoing, hands-on learning with students.
